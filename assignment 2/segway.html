Segway'n USA

Documenting my trip around the US on my very own Segway!

June 2, 2005

My first day of the trip! I can't believe I finally got everything packed and ready to go. Because I'm on a Segway, I wasn't able to bring a whole lot with me:

  • Cellphone
  • iPod
  • Digital camera
  • Protein bar
Just the essentials. As Lau Tzu would have said, "A journey of a thousand miles begins with one stop Segway."

July 14, 2005

I saw some Burma Shave style signs on the side of the road today:

"Passing cars, when you can't see, May get you, A glimpse of eternity." I definitely won't be passing any cars!"

August 20, 2005

Well I made it 1200 miles already, and I passed through some interesting places on the way:

  1. Walla Walla, WA
  2. Magie City, ID
  3. Bountiful, UT
  4. Last Chance, CO
  5. Why, AZ
  6. Truth or Consequences, NM
